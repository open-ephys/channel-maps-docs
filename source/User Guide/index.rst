Important considerations
==============================

In Bonsai, the channel numbering starts at 0 since it follows the numbering of the Intan chip channels.
In the Open Ephys GUI, the channel numbering starts at 1. Make sure you add 1 to the Intan channel numberings provided in this documentation.
